How we can help you

Hyperparameter is a consultancy specialising in applying machine learning and data science to solve business problems.

How we can transform your business:

  • Reduce costs by applying machine learning to automate repetitive tasks, reducing manual labour.
  • Increase revenue by improving product recommendations to increase sales.
  • Reduce customer acquisition costs by running experiments.
  • Create new products by leveraging your companies data to generate insight.

We specialise in:

  • Machine Learning
  • Data Science
  • Text Mining
  • Natural Language Processing

Hyperparameter is founded by Daoud Clarke, a data scientist at Lumi. He has been working in industry for seven years applying machine learning, natural language processing and optimisation techniques to real-world problems, building on knowledge obtained in his PhD and post-doc in computational linguistics.
